## QUICK REFERENCE

### When to Roll
- GM calls for it (usually when outcome uncertain)
- Difficulty 3+ for most tasks
- Add relevant stat to 4dF roll

### When to Invoke Aspects
- Spend 1 Fate Point for +2 or reroll
- Must narratively justify how aspect helps
- Maximum drama > mechanical optimization

### When to Take Stress
- GM assigns stress based on threat
- Choose track that makes narrative sense
- If track full, take consequence instead

### When Fronts Advance
- End of scenes (GM discretion)
- When you take actions that trigger them
- Between sessions (time passage)
- You can't stop all fronts (that's the point)
