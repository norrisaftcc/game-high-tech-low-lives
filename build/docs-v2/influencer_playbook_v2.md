## TALENTS (Choose 2)

### ☐ Performance Mode (4 points)
When working with audience watching, +2 to Tech or Cool (your choice). You're better when people are watching. Once per session, call for your followers' help—they crowdsource solution, find information, or provide distraction. GM determines what they can do.

### ☐ Sponsored Access (2 points)
Your sponsors want you in interesting places. Once per session, declare your sponsors arranged access to a location, event, or person. It's legitimate—you're there to create content. This comes with obligation to produce something they want.

### ☐ Viral Moment (2 points)
When you succeed with style, roll Cool. On success, this goes viral—your followers spread it, you gain temporary reputation boost. Use this for +2 to next social roll in same location. Your fame precedes you.

### ☐ Sponsor Leverage (2 points)
Your sponsors are powerful. Once per session, invoke corporate backing for +2 to negotiation or access. "I work for Meridian Holdings" opens doors. This creates obligation—sponsors will want something in return.

### ☐ Audience Feedback (2 points)
Your followers watch and comment in real-time. When analyzing problem, roll Cool—on success, someone in your audience has relevant expertise and helps. On failure, they're confidently wrong and you waste time following bad advice.

### ☐ Content Vault (2 points)
You've recorded everything. Once per session, review past streams for perfect recall of any scene you've streamed. You have video evidence of what happened—if you want to release it.

## THE INFLUENCER MOVE: THE STREAM

Once per session, when you activate your stream for a critical moment, you can invoke your audience:

**"Fifty thousand people are watching this right now."**

Everyone in the scene knows they're being watched by your followers. This changes behavior:
- Corporate security can't be too heavy-handed (bad PR)
- Criminals might play to the camera (or flee from exposure)
- Bystanders want to be on camera (help or hinder)
- Your sponsors are watching (they'll have opinions)

While The Stream is active:
- You gain +2 to Cool (performing for audience)
- Evidence exists of everything that happens
- You can't back down without losing followers
- Complications come from being public

After the scene, roll Cool:
- **Success**: This goes viral. Your reputation grows. Gain Fate point.
- **Failure**: Controversy. Comments are brutal. Sponsor wants to talk.
