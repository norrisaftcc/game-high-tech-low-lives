# SHODANN: SOLO PLAY GUIDE
## Deep Cover Infiltration for One Player

*Play the prequel. Establish the character. Test the cover. Before the crew arrives, Ann Sho operates alone.*

---

## WHAT IS THIS?

This is a complete solo play system for running **Shodann's infiltration** of AlgoCratic-Fujikara. Play one character through corporate espionage, identity management, and resistance operations. No GM required - the fronts, relationship maps, and decision trees create pressure and story.

**You'll play**: Ann Sho (aka Sable Harker, aka Samantha O'Day) - g0 operative with three identities, teaching students by day, running dead drops by night, monitored by an emergent AI that might be becoming conscious.

## THE STRUCTURE

This solo play system uses:

**Fronts** (Apocalypse World) - Threats with countdown clocks that advance unless stopped
**Relationship Maps** (Ron Edwards) - Human connections that create drama
**Phase Trio** (FATE) - Character generation through history and relationships
**Decision Trees** - Structured choices that branch based on your actions
**Solo GM Techniques** - Oracles, random tables, and pressure systems

**Together**: A complete solo experience where your choices matter and consequences accumulate.

## WHY SOLO PLAY?

**Before crew play begins**, establish Shodann's situation:
- Cover established but fragile
- Students awakening to resistance
- Digital clone diverging from source
- G0 network compromised and collapsing
- Manager Chen watching, testing, judging

**Solo prequel creates**: History, pressure, relationships, and consequences that carry into crew play.

**Or play standalone**: Complete infiltration arc from insertion to extraction (or exposure).

## WHAT YOU'LL EXPERIENCE

**Corporate espionage** - Maintain three identities while infiltrating mega-corp
**Teaching resistance** - Guide students toward liberation or protect them from consequences
**Surveillance state** - Every action monitored by emergent AI learning to be conscious
**Identity crisis** - Which Ann Sho is real when you've performed for so long?
**Countdown pressure** - Four fronts advancing, forcing choices between missions

**Tone**: Gene Wolfe meets Mr. Robot. Elegant paranoia. Smart competence under existential pressure.

## DESIGN NOTES

This solo system combines:
- Vincent Baker's Fronts (threats in motion)
- Ron Edwards' R-Maps (relationship drama)
- FATE's Phase Trio (connected history)
- PbtA's principles (play to find out what happens)

**It's not a branching story.** It's a pressure system. Fronts advance. NPCs want things. Your choices slow some threats, accelerate others. You can't stop everything. That's the point.

**Success isn't winning.** Success is interesting choices under pressure.

---

*"You're not infiltrating a corporation. You're infiltrating a web of relationships while four countdown clocks tick toward crisis. Choose which fires to fight. Accept the consequences of the ones you let burn."*
