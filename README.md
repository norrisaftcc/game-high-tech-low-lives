## What Is This?

A rules-light cyberpunk roleplaying game combining Fate mechanics with Powered by the Apocalypse playbooks. Set in a world 93% covered by ocean, where corporations control platform cities, chrome is ubiquitous, and identity is fluid.

**Core Premise**: Corporate dystopia. Body modification. Surveillance state. Competent characters under pressure. Every augmentation has a cost.

**Tone**: Gene Wolfe meets Mr. Robot. Elegant paranoia. Smart people making hard choices.

## System Overview

- **Stats**: Body, Reflexes, Code, Tech, Cool (+0 to +3)
- **Rolling**: 4 Fate dice + stat vs. difficulty or opposition
- **Stress**: Meat, Nerves, Systems (3 boxes each, modified by chrome)
- **Aspects**: High Concept, Trouble, Connection (spend Fate points to invoke)
- **Build Points**: 8 points for talents, training, gear, or natural advantage

Full mechanics in [Core Rules](/core-rules/index.md).
