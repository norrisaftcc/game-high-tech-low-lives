**Setting**: Corporate cyberpunk in a pelagic (93% ocean-covered) world
**Format**: Print-and-play, self-contained playbooks (2-4 pages each)

## Game Mechanics Quick Reference

### Stats (Rated +0 to +3)
- **Body**: Physical endurance, strength, resilience
- **Reflexes**: Speed, precision, reaction time
- **Code**: Digital intrusion, programming, system manipulation
- **Tech**: Hardware engineering, repair, jury-rigging
- **Cool**: Nerve, presence, emotional control

### Stress Tracks
- **Meat**: Physical damage (base 3 boxes)
- **Nerves**: Psychological strain (base 3 boxes)
- **Systems**: Technical damage to chrome (base 3 boxes)

Chrome shifts these tracks (+Systems often costs -Meat or -Nerves)

### Consequences
- **Mild** (2 shifts): Clears after one scene
- **Moderate** (4 shifts): Requires attention to clear
- **Severe** (6 shifts): Permanent until bought off with advancement

### Core Roll
Roll 4 Fate dice (showing +, -, or blank)
Add relevant stat modifier
Compare to difficulty or opposing roll

**Results**:
- **10+**: Strong success, you get what you want
- **7-9**: Success with cost, complication, or hard choice
- **6-**: Failure or GM move (things get worse)

### Aspects
- **High Concept**: Primary identity
- **Trouble**: What complicates your life
- **Connection**: Someone who matters to you

Invoke (spend Fate point for +2) or be compelled (GM offers Fate point for complication)

## Working on This Project

### Before Starting
1. Read [Writing Style Guide](../../conventions/writing-style.md)
2. Review [Design Principles](../../conventions/design-principles.md)
3. Check existing playbooks for format/tone consistency
4. Review [Git Workflow](../../conventions/git-workflow.md)

### Content Checklist
- [ ] Matches established playbook format (see playbooks.md)
- [ ] Chrome options show clear stress trade-offs
- [ ] Moves have clear triggers and outcomes (10+/7-9/6-)
- [ ] Examples show rules in action (don't explain)
- [ ] Tone is terse for mechanics, evocative for flavor
- [ ] Terminology is consistent across playbooks
- [ ] Print-friendly (2-4 pages)
