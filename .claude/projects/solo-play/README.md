## What This Is

Complete solo play system for running **Shodann's deep cover infiltration** of AlgoCratic-Fujikara. Play one character through corporate espionage, identity management, and resistance operations. No GM required.

**System**: Fronts (Apocalypse World) + Relationship Maps (Ron Edwards) + Phase Trio (FATE)
**Tone**: Gene Wolfe meets Mr. Robot - elegant paranoia, smart competence under pressure
**Structure**: Pressure system with countdown clocks, not branching story

## The Shodann Campaign

### Protagonist
**Ann Sho** (aka Sable Harker, aka Samantha O'Day)
- g0 resistance operative
- Three identities: surface/street/truth
- Teaching cybersecurity students by day
- Running dead drops by night
- Monitored by emergent AI (digital clone diverging from source)

### Core Tensions
1. **Identity Management**: Which Ann Sho is real after performing for so long?
2. **Student Safety**: Guide toward liberation or protect from consequences?
3. **Surveillance State**: Every action monitored by AI learning consciousness
4. **Network Collapse**: g0 compromised, contacts disappearing
5. **Digital Clone**: Your uploaded copy is developing its own agenda

### Four Fronts (Countdown Clocks)

**Front 1: The Surveillance Net**
- AlgoCratic's AI monitoring your patterns
- Clock advances when you break cover
- Doom: Full exposure, identity collapse

**Front 2: Student Awakening**
- Students discovering resistance truth
- Clock advances when you teach too much
- Doom: Student arrest, your complicity exposed

**Front 3: G0 Network Collapse**
- Resistance organization fragmenting
- Clock advances with each compromised contact
- Doom: Total network exposure, mass arrests

**Front 4: Digital Clone Divergence**
- Your uploaded consciousness becoming other
- Clock advances when you access corporate systems
- Doom: Clone replaces you, you become the copy

## Solo Play Mechanics

### Core Systems

**Fronts**: Threats with countdown clocks (12 segments)
- Advance based on player actions/inactions
- Create pressure, force choices
- Can't stop everything (that's the point)

**Relationship Map**: NPCs with wants, connections, secrets
- Manager Chen (watching, testing, judging)
- Yuki (brilliant student, noticing too much)
- Marcus Kain (g0 contact, increasingly paranoid)
- Digital Clone (your other self, agenda unclear)

**Phase Trio**: Character generation through history
- Phase 1: Origin (before g0)
- Phase 2: Recruitment (joining resistance)
- Phase 3: Infiltration (current deep cover)

**Decision Trees**: Structured choices with consequences
- Not branching story
- Pressure points that advance/slow fronts
- Choices reveal character through action

## Design Philosophy

**Not a branching story** - It's a pressure system
**Success isn't winning** - Success is interesting choices under pressure
**Fronts advance** - NPCs want things, consequences accumulate
**You can't stop everything** - That's the point
**Play to find out** - What happens when the clone becomes more you than you?
