## CHROME (Choose 2)

Each piece of chrome shifts your stress tracks. Some give you capacity. Some take it away. Choose carefully.

### ☐ Neural Deck Port
*Direct brain-to-system interface. No external hardware needed.*
- **Effect**: +1 to Code when directly connected to systems
- **Stress Shift**: +2 Systems, -1 Meat
- **Maintenance**: 400 credits/month
- **When it breaks**: You can't jack in. Roll Code at -2 until repaired.

### ☐ Pattern Recognition Suite  
*Enhanced visual processing. You see connections others miss.*
- **Effect**: +2 to Code when analyzing systems for vulnerabilities
- **Stress Shift**: +1 Systems, -1 Nerves (information overload)
- **Maintenance**: 300 credits/month
- **When it breaks**: Migraine-level sensory input. Take 1 Nerves stress per scene until fixed.

### ☐ Subvocal Comm
*Silent communication via throat vibrations. Looks like thinking.*
- **Effect**: Communicate without being overheard. Encrypted channel.
- **Stress Shift**: None (minimal installation)
- **Maintenance**: 100 credits/month
- **When it breaks**: You revert to speaking out loud. Compel this when bad timing.

### ☐ Memory Buffer
*External storage for experiences. Perfect recall when needed.*
- **Effect**: Once per session, remember any scene perfectly. Review it frame by frame.
- **Stress Shift**: +1 Systems, -1 Nerves (disconnection from present)
- **Maintenance**: 250 credits/month
- **When it breaks**: Your memories feel unreliable. -1 to Cool until repaired.

### ☐ Reflex Boosters
*Accelerated reaction time. Sometimes too fast.*
- **Effect**: +1 to Reflexes for quick response
- **Stress Shift**: +1 Systems, -1 Nerves (react before thinking)
- **Maintenance**: 400 credits/month
- **When it breaks**: Delayed responses. Take -2 to Reflexes until fixed.

## TALENTS (Choose 2)

### ☐ Backdoor Specialist (4 points)
Once per session, automatically succeed at infiltrating a system you've previously audited. No roll needed. You left yourself a way in.

### ☐ Corporate Camouflage (2 points)
+2 to Cool when maintaining cover in corporate environments. You know how to look busy while doing nothing.

### ☐ Quick Exit Protocol (2 points)
When caught somewhere you shouldn't be, roll Cool. On success, you have a plausible excuse ready. On failure, you still get out but someone remembers you.

### ☐ Information Broker (2 points)
You've got contacts. Once per session, declare you know someone who has the information you need. GM says who and what it costs.

### ☐ Emergency Purge (2 points)
When Systems stress fills, clear all Systems stress by taking Moderate Consequence: *Chrome Shutdown*. One augment goes dark until repaired.

### ☐ Teaching Through Pain (2 points)
When you exploit someone's security flaw, they gain XP toward fixing that vulnerability. You're weaponizing education.

## HOW TO PLAY THE INFILTRATOR

### YOU'RE GOOD AT:
- Getting into places you shouldn't be
- Finding vulnerabilities in systems
- Maintaining cover under scrutiny  
- Gathering information nobody wants you to have
- Looking like you belong when you absolutely don't

### YOU'RE BAD AT:
- Direct confrontation (you're a ghost, not a soldier)
- Staying off corporate databases (your access leaves traces)
- Trusting people (everyone's a potential security risk)
- Not overthinking the job (you see every possible failure)

### WHEN YOU:
- **Infiltrate a system**: Roll Code. Success means you're in. Failure means you're in but they know it.
- **Maintain cover**: Roll Cool. Success means you're believable. Failure means someone's suspicious.
- **Find vulnerabilities**: Roll Code or Tech depending on approach. Success reveals weakness. Failure reveals you.
- **Get caught**: Roll Cool or Reflexes to talk or run your way out. Failure means consequences.

### YOUR PLAYBOOK MOVE

**I Know This System**: When you analyze security you've encountered before, roll Code. On 10+, choose three. On 7-9, choose one:
- You know exactly where the weak point is (+2 to infiltration)
- You know who designed it (and their typical mistakes)
- You know the backup system that activates if primary fails
- You left a backdoor last time (automatic entry once)
